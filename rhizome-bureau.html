Rhizome Bureau / Architecture / Digital

Hide

RHIZOME is an architectural practice based in Tel Aviv, founded in 2011. They specialize in the creation of public spaces and social projects of various types and scales, including landscapes and buildings. Their work aims to have an immediate impact on everyday life while contributing to the development of a sustainable urban environment.

We have designed a website that reflects this approach, effectively showcasing RHIZOME’s projects in a manner that is informative yet not overwhelming for viewers, highlighting their key features and contributions.

Credits:

Vasilii Podriadchikov (creative direction, design)
Denis Valetin (digital design)
Vlad Danilchenko (Front-end)
Pavel Danilchenko (Back-end)
Alex Bazan (client success management)
